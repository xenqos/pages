# Electrical Transients
<p>This chapter explores the response of capacitors and inductors to sudden changes in DC voltage (called a <em>transient</em> voltage), when wired <a href="https://www.allaboutcircuits.com/video-lectures/series-circuits-part-1/" target="_blank">in series</a> with a <a href="https://www.allaboutcircuits.com/video-lectures/electronic-components-resistors/" target="_blank">resistor</a>. Unlike resistors, which respond instantaneously to applied voltage, capacitors and inductors react over time as they absorb and release energy.<a name="Transient"></a></p>
