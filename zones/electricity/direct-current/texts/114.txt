# Electrical Transients
<p>This chapter explores the response of capacitors and inductors to sudden changes in DC voltage (called a <em>transient</em> voltage), when wired in series with a resistor. Unlike resistors, which respond instantaneously to applied voltage, capacitors and inductors react over time as they absorb and release energy.</p>
