# Recitation

### pranava japa

[|
|pranava|प्रणव |AUM ॐ|
|meru	  |मेरु	|summit|
|akṣa	  |अक्ष	|eye (bead)|
|sūtra	|सूत्र	|thread|
|]

## rudaraksha mala
[-
108 beads
1 meru
-]
^[rudaraksha-mala.webp]

## Left Hand
[-
executes chin mudra
index finger makes a loop and touches base of thumb
middle, ring, little fingers stretched
palm up
rests on left knee
-]
^[chin-mudra.webp]

## Right Hand
[-
counts mala beads
mala is on middle finger
thumb moves beads
hand is on right knee
mala hangs on inner side of leg
-]
^[pranava-japa.webp]

## Posture
[-
sit on chair
sit facing east
spine straight
lean forward a bit
-]

## Eyes
[-
eyes closed
eyes crossed on innerbrow / tip of nose
-]

## Tongue
[-
tongue on palate
-]

## Execution
[-
index finger does not touch bead
thumb pulls bead toward body
recite AUM
recite three sounds A, U, M
meru not to be crossed
after reaching meru, rotate mala
-]
